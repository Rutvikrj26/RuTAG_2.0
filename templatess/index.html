I.I.T Delhi

It is located in eight IITs (Bombay, Delhi, Guwahati, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Madras, Roorkee, Ropar). The RuTAG cells are coordinated by the Office of the Principal Scientific Adviser (PSA) to the Govt. of India, New Delhi

  What we do?

The main work of the RuTAG is to identify the loose ends in the technologies at the local level and provide a techinal upgrade to those technologies thus enabling a better livelihood and safety for the rural community using that technology

 Our Aim

Rural technology support is critical for realizing the vast potential of the rural farm and non-farm sectors. Keeping the above aspects in view, the Office of Principal Scientific Advisor (PSA) to the Government of India conceptualised a mission called Rural Technology Action Group (RuTAG). The RuTAG was conceived as a mechanism to provide a higher level of S&T intervention and support. This intervention, which is essentially demand-driven, could be for technology up-gradation, hi-tech delivery, technology training and demonstration or through any other innovative method. Specific training programmes are also sponsored by RuTAG.

 Where are we?

The Office of the Principal Scientific Advisor to the Government of India has currently established 7 RuTAG centres located at 7 IITs namely IIT Delhi, IIT Kanpur, IIT Bombay, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Madras, IIT Guwahati, and IIT Roorkee throughout the country.

Here is our Course of Action

Regional Workshop Problem Identification Project Evaluation Project Development Business Proposal Technology Dissemination
RuTAG IIT Delhi organizes a regional workshop twice or thrice a year with rural people. Several NGOs and rural people from that region attend the workshop.
NGOs and local people come up with the technical problems which needs to be rectified in that region in order to boost their livelihood.
RuTAG Core Committee selects the problems related to technology improvements which can be rectified by RuTAG. This also needs approval from the Office of the PSA.
After the project initiation, project design and development process begin. Field agency is the direct contact for the inputs for better understanding of problem and for the field trials from the beginning of the prototyping of the technology.
Business Proposal for the develped technologies is proposed and the prosals are approved for General Public Welfare.
After the satisfactory results of the improved technology tested in field, it is considered as ready to be disseminated. Field agency have the responsibility for the dissemination of the technology.
